About Me

My name is Matt. Smiles. I am a father of three; Adrian, William & Anna. Depending on their mood, I will refer to them as either my three little angels or my three little animals. My wife Laura and I live with William & Anna in Sartell, MN. Adrian lives with his mother in a Twin Cities suburb.

I am a Manager for Life Link III, an air ambulance company based in Minneapolis, MN. I manage three bases that make up the 'West Region'; Alexandria, Brainerd & Willmar, MN. I've been a paramedic for about ten years and a manager for the last two years.

I'm currenly enrolled in a coding 'bootcamp' with the goal of "learning how to learn" coding.
